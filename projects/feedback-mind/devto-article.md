# I Built an AI Tool to Analyze User Feedback - Here's What I Learned

As an indie hacker, I know the struggle: your users are giving you feedback everywhere - emails, Twitter, Intercom, support tickets. But there's just **too much** to read.

So I decided to do something about it.

## The Problem

You're busy building products. You don't have time to:
- Read 100+ user emails
- Categorize feedback into bugs, features, complaints
- Figure out what to build next

That's where **FeedbackMind AI** comes in.

## What I Built

**FeedbackMind AI** automatically:
- Categorizes feedback (bugs, features, complaints, compliments)
- Scores priorities based on sentiment + frequency
- Summarizes key insights in seconds

## Target Audience

Currently focused on indie hackers with **10-1,000 users** - people who have enough feedback to analyze but don't have a team to do it for them.

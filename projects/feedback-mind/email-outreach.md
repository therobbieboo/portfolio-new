# Cold Email Outreach - FeedbackMind

## 策略
找到有類似痛點的潛在用戶，直接聯繫

## 目標客戶
- Indie hackers with Twitter/社交媒體 presence
- Product Hunt 上的新創作者
- 有產品但沒有系統化收集反饋的人

---

## Email 1: 初次接觸

**Subject:** Quick question about [their product]

Hey [Name],

I noticed you're building [their product] - really cool!

I was wondering: how do you handle user feedback? Do you have a system for tracking what users want?

I ask because I just launched a small tool that uses AI to analyze user feedback automatically. It saves indie hackers about 5+ hours per week.

Would you be interested in trying it for free? No catch - I'm just looking for feedback.

Cheers,
[Your name]

---

## Email 2: 跟進（如果沒回覆）

**Subject:** (2/3) Just checking in

Hey [Name],

Just following up on my last email - no pressure at all!

If feedback analysis isn't a pain point for you right now, no worries.

But if it ever becomes one, I'd love to chat: https://feedbackmind.app

Cheers!

---

## Twitter DM 版本

更短、更直接：

**DM 1:**
> Hey! I built a free tool that analyzes user feedback with AI. Thought you might find it useful since you're building [their product]. Check it out if you're interested: feedbackmind.app

## 追蹤系統

| 階段 | 動作 | 時間 |
|------|------|------|
| Day 0 | 發送 Email 1 | - |
| Day 3 | 發送 Email 2 | 如果沒回覆 |
| Day 7 | 最後 follow | 如果沒回覆 |
| Day 14 | 移除從未回覆的名單 | - |

# Follow-up Sequence - Day 1 to Day 7

## Day 0 - Launch Day
**Goal:** Get first signups

### If no signups after 2 hours:
- [ ] Send reminder to Luke to post on Reddit
- [ ] Post in our own networks (if any)

### If 1-5 signups:
- [ ] Send welcome email to new users
- [ ] Ask for quick feedback

---

## Day 1-3 - Validation Phase
**Goal:** Confirm product-market fit

### Daily Tasks:
1. Check signups
2. Send personalized welcome email to each new user
3. Ask 1: "Would you pay for this? How much?"
4. Ask 2: "What's the #1 thing missing?"

### Email Template - Welcome
```
Subject: Welcome to FeedbackMind! 🎉

Hi [Name],

Thanks for signing up! 

I'm building FeedbackMind to help indie hackers like you stop guessing what to build next.

Quick question: What's the biggest challenge you face with user feedback right now?

Reply and let me know - I'd love to hear your thoughts!

Cheers,
[Your name]
Founder, FeedbackMind
```

---

## Day 4-7 - Iteration Phase
**Goal:** Get first paying customer

### If users love it:
- [ ] Offer early adopter discount (50% off)
- [ ] Ask for testimonial

### If users have concerns:
- [ ] Address top 3 concerns
- [ ] Update product if needed

### Outreach to warm leads:
- [ ] Email people who showed interest but didn't sign up
- [ ] "Hey, I made that change you mentioned..."

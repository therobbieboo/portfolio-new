## 💬 評論策略（關鍵！）

根據數據：Top 產品的評論數是平均的 **9.5 倍**

### Launch Day 任務
1. 準備 20+ 條預設評論
2. 動員 10+ 朋友幫評論
3. 每小時回覆新評論
4. 主動在評論區問問題

### 評論模板
- "This is exactly what I need! 🔥"
- "How does this compare to [competitor]?"
- "Love the simplicity. Will this work for mobile apps too?"
- "Just signed up! Can't wait to try it."
- "Been waiting for something like this."
